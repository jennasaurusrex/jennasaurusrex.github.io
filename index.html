All About Me!

My background stems from the road less traveled. I've spent most of my time taking the reigns of visual management at my job with Starbucks, and even though I have been dabbling in front-end development for over ten years recreationally, I took the plunge into General Assembly, targeting a higher tier of education. A touch over a year ago, I took a leave of absense to spend six months hiking the Appalachian Trail. I firmly believe this was one of the most wild and perhaps the most crucial learning experiences of my life. What I realized through those grueling six months is that I not only have a desire to continue learning well past college or any standard job, but that I have a hunger for it. Driving excellence and delivering value are my job. I believe the best opportunities are created, not assigned, and that my job is to be a high-performing value creator.
